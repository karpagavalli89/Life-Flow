Who We Are & What We Do

LifeFlow is a state-of-the-art decentralized blood management platform designed to eliminate blood shortages. We streamline everything from storage optimization to donor mobilization via cutting-edge web infrastructure.

Our mission is simple: to make sure no medical emergency fails due to the unavailability of safe, verified blood inventory.

  • Real-time inventory synchronization
  • Seamless hospital-to-bank API pipelines
  • Strictly monitored temperature control networks

How The Donation Process Works

Giving blood is safe, simple, and takes less than an hour of your time.

01

Registration

Fill out a basic medical questionnaire and verification process at our counter or online portal.

02

Mini-Physical

A quick check of your pulse, blood pressure, body temperature, and hemoglobin levels to ensure safety.

03

The Donation

The actual blood draw takes only about 8-10 minutes under highly sterile conditions overseen by professionals.

04

Refreshment

Enjoy a brief snack and drinks in our recovery lounge to restore your hydration levels before you leave.
